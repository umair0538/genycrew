About GenYCrew

GenYCrew is a young and energetic tech startup. We are a team of hackers who love working with latest tech stack to develop software applications for our clients all over the world. Our crew members have combined experience of over two decades working on top games, apps, and websites. We have experience working with clients from US, Europe and Middle East.

We use Scrum methodology to maintain agility. Daily status updates are shared with clients for full visibility. We believe in continous integration and unit testing to develop quality software with minimal effort.

Please don't hesitate in reaching out to us if you have a product idea or have any kind of software need for your business.
